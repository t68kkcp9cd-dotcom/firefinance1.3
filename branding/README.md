### Design Philosophy

The Fire Finance brand combines the warmth and energy of fire with the trust and professionalism required for financial management. The flame symbol represents:

- **Growth** - Rising flames symbolize financial progress
- **Energy** - Dynamic approach to personal finance
- **Warmth** - Approachable, family-friendly design
- **Transformation** - Turning financial chaos into clarity
